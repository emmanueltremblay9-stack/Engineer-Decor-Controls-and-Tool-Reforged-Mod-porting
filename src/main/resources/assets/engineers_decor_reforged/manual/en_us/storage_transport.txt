Storage & Transport
Inventory and flow fittings
Storage and transport fittings handle crates, barrels, funnels, and valves for compact factory routing.<br>
<br>
Use these pages when laying out stockrooms, pipe runs, service tanks, and controlled fluid lines.
<np>
Labeled Crate
Editable stock label
<&labeled_crate>This labeled crate is built for clear stockroom sorting. Its front plate can be edited like a small workshop sign.<br>
<br>
Usage: Right-click the label plate to edit the crate label. Right-click the rest of the crate to open storage for fittings, parts, and maintenance supplies.<br>
<np>
Fluid Barrel
Fluid line hardware
<&fluid_barrel>This fitting is a compact fluid-line part for small tanks, pipe runs, collection points, and redstone-controlled flow.<br>
<br>
Usage: Place it where pipes or buckets can reach the tank face.<br>

Fluid Funnel
Fluid line hardware

<br>
