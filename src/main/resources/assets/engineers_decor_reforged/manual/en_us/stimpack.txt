Auto Stim Pack
Emergency injector
<&recipe>The Auto Stim Pack is an emergency recovery item for dangerous maintenance work.<br>
<br>
Usage: Use it manually to heal, gain brief protection, and get a short movement boost. When carried in inventory, it can also trigger automatically at low health, then enters cooldown.<br>
<br>
Keep one available before testing high places, exposed wiring, or hostile mine routes.
