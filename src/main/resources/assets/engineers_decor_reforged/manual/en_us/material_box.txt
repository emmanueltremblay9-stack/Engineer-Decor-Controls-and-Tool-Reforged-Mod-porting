Material Storage Box
Construction supply box
<&recipe>The Material Storage Box carries a large reserve of one plain, unmodified stackable construction material.<br>
<br>
Usage: Hold the box in the main hand and a material stack in the offhand to load that material into the box. Use the box with an empty offhand to pull one stack back out.<br>
<br>
The box holds up to 512 items, keeps only one material type at a time, rejects nested boxes, non-stackable gear, and modified named or data-bearing stacks, and reports its stored contents in the tooltip.
