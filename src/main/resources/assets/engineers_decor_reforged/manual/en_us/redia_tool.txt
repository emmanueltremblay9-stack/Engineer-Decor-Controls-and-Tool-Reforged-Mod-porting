REDIA Tool
Diamond-covered utility
<&recipe>The REDIA Tool is a durable field utility for quick ground work around temporary sites and mine entrances.<br>
<br>
Usage: Use it on grass, dirt, coarse dirt, rooted dirt, or podzol with clear air above to till farmland. Sneak-use it against a stable side or top face while carrying torches to place one torch from your inventory.<br>
<br>
Each successful tilling or torch placement damages the tool.
