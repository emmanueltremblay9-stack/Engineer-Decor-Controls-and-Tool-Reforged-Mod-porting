Muslee Bar Press
Food press
<&recipe>The Muslee Bar Press turns common food supplies into compact field rations.<br>
<br>
Usage: Carry bread, an apple, and wheat seeds, then use the press. A successful press consumes one of each ingredient, checks that the output can fit, creates four Muslee Melange Power Bars, and damages the press.<br>
<br>
If an ingredient is missing or the inventory has no room for the bars, nothing is consumed.
