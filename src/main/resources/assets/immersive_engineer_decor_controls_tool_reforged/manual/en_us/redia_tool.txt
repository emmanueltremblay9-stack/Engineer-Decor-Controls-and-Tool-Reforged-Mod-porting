REDIA Tool
Diamond-covered utility
<&recipe>The REDIA Tool is a durable diamond-grade multi-tool for field work around temporary sites, workshops, and mine entrances.<br>
<br>
Usage: Use it while carrying torches to place one torch from your inventory. Sneak-use it to shear plants, cycle ground between dirt, farmland, coarse dirt, and dirt path, or strip logs. Sneak while breaking logs to fell connected tree blocks.<br>
<br>
Repair it with diamonds. Over-repairing a fully repaired REDIA Tool restores the original Efficiency and Fortune progression.
