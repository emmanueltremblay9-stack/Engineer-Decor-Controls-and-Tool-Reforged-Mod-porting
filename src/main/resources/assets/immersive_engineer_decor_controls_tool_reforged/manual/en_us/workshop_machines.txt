Plate workstation

<br>

Factory Hopper
Shop-floor utility
<&factory_hopper>This block moves item stacks through a tighter industrial casing.<br>
<br>
Usage: Face it toward the inventory line, then set range, stack count, timing, and redstone mode in its screen.<br>

Factory Dropper
Shop-floor utility
<&factory_dropper>This block releases stored items from an aimed face.<br>
<br>
