Clinker & Masonry
Brick and concrete shells
Clinker, slag brick, reinforced concrete, and panzer glass provide heavy surfaces for hot rooms, workshops, and protected sightlines.<br>
<br>
Use the matching slabs, stairs, slices, and walls to finish edges without changing the factory material language.
<np>

Soot-dark brickwork
<&clinker_brick_block>This block brings soot-darkened brickwork to furnace halls, chimney bases, and service walls.<br>
<br>
Usage: Use it as a wall, trim, or heat-zone shell around furnaces and boilers.<br>
