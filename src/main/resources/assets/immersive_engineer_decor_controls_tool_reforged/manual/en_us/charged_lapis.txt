Charged Lapis
Stored experience
<&recipe>Charged Lapis stores one experience level created through the Mind Squeezer.<br>
<br>
Usage: Consume it to restore one level, clear active effects, and recover a small amount of health.<br>
<br>
The recipe widget shows the Mind Squeezer because Charged Lapis is produced by using that tool with lapis and XP available.
